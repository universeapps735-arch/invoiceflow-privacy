Privacy Policy for InvoiceFlow

Last updated: April 10, 2026

1. Introduction

InvoiceFlow is a GST invoice management app for Indian businesses. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

2. Information You Enter

All business information you enter — including company name, GST number, PAN, address, phone, email, bank details, and invoice data — is stored only on your device using local storage. We do not collect, transmit, or have access to any of this information. It never leaves your phone unless you choose to share it yourself.

3. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect:

  • Device advertising ID
  • IP address
  • Device model and OS version
  • App usage information

This data is collected by Google and governed by Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy. We do not receive or store any of this data ourselves.

4. Photos / Images

The app allows you to upload a business logo and signatory image from your device gallery. These images are stored locally on your device only and are never uploaded to any server.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with anyone. The only third party that receives any data is Google AdMob as described above.

6. Data Security

All your invoice and business data stays on your device. You are in full control — you can export, delete, or clear it at any time through the app.

7. Children's Privacy

InvoiceFlow is a professional business tool intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted at this URL.
